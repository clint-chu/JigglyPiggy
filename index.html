About:

Oh no ! Piggy Bankers needs help getting his money back! Watch out for bombs as Piggy Bankers is easily startled !

How to play:

- Drag and drop coins into Piggy Bankers without hitting a bomb

- Press "spacebar" to pause

- Press "e" to start/restart

- Click the music icon to turn music on or off
